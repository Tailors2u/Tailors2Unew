Frequently Asked Questions

Simply book a time that works for you. Your chosen tailor will arrive at your home or office with fabric samples. They will take your measurements professionally and discuss your style preferences to ensure a perfect fit and design.

Typically, your custom-made garment will be crafted and delivered within 7-10 business days after your measurement appointment. For complex orders like bridal wear, the timeline might be slightly longer, which your tailor will confirm during the consultation.

Our tailors carry a wide range of curated fabric samples. If you don't find what you're looking for, there's no obligation to proceed. The initial consultation is complimentary. You can also describe what you want, and we can source specific fabrics for you.

Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer a 'Perfect Fit Guarantee'. If your garment requires minor adjustments after delivery, we will provide one round of alterations free of charge within 15 days of delivery.

The final cost depends on the fabric you choose and the complexity of the garment. Our pricing is transparent: it's a combination of the fabric cost (per meter) and a stitching fee for the garment type (e.g., shirt, suit, dress). Your tailor will provide a detailed quote for your approval before any work begins.
